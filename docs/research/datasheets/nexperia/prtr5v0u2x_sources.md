## Extracted Board-Level Facts

The saved Nexperia datasheet identifies `PRTR5V0U2X` as a SOT143B
rail-to-rail ESD protection diode for two high-speed data lines. The pinout is
`GND`, `I/O 1`, `I/O 2`, and `VCC`.

The modeled static validation facts are:

- reverse standoff voltage: `5.5 V` maximum,
- I/O-to-ground capacitance: `1.0 pF` typical, `1.5 pF` maximum,
- I/O-to-I/O capacitance: `0.6 pF` typical,
- supply-pin-to-ground capacitance: `16 pF` typical.
